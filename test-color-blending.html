Color Blending Game

This game has been successfully added to the Edukiz application.

Features:

  • 12 Colors: Red, Blue, Yellow, Green, Orange, Purple, Pink, Brown, Black, White, Cyan, Magenta
  • Bilingual Sounds: English and Filipino color names with sound effects
  • Drag & Drop: Drag colors to mixing slots
  • Color Mixing: Automatic color mixing with 16 predefined combinations
  • Mixed Color Sounds: Sounds for mixed colors (e.g., "red and blue makes purple")
  • Color Identification Game: Additional game to identify colors
  • 4 Difficulty Levels: Progressive difficulty with more colors and mixing slots
  • Mobile Optimized: Responsive design for all devices
